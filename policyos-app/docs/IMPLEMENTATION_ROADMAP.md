# PolicyOS Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Foundation
- FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, Alembic
- Organization, user, membership, RBAC
- Audit events, secrets, configuration

## Phase 2 — Policy Core
- Strategic goals
- Policy candidates and screening
- Assessments and readiness levels
- Portfolios, agenda and dependencies

## Phase 3 — Delivery
- Missions, tasks, milestones and RACI
- Decision gates, issues, risks, escalation
- Deliverables and operational handover

## Phase 4 — Intelligence
- Evidence registry and knowledge graph
- Legislative and government-program tracking
- Domestic/global benchmarking and watchlists

## Phase 5 — AI Office
- Jamie orchestrator
- Policy, legal, budget, media, statistics agents
- Human review queues and confidence controls

## Phase 6 — Experience and Operations
- Next.js dashboard, mobile and voice
- Observability, backups, DR and DevSecOps
- Production deployment and security hardening
