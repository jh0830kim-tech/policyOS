# Project Charter

## Product name
PolicyOS

## Product statement
PolicyOS is a secure AI Office platform that coordinates people, documents, data, workflows, and specialized AI agents for public-policy work.

## Primary users
- Elected officials
- Legislative assistants
- Policy researchers
- Legal reviewers
- Communications staff
- Budget and statistics analysts
- Administrative staff

## Problems to solve
- Repetitive document work
- Fragmented institutional knowledge
- Slow policy research
- Inconsistent review processes
- Weak traceability of AI-generated output
- Difficulty coordinating specialist work

## Product principles
- Public value first
- Human authorization for consequential actions
- Source-aware AI
- Secure-by-default architecture
- Modular, testable services
- Incremental delivery

## Initial success criteria
- Users authenticate securely.
- Role permissions are enforced.
- Policy work is tracked by organization.
- AI-generated outputs preserve evidence and review state.
- Official actions are auditable.
