# Glossary

- **Agent**: A specialized AI role with defined tools, permissions, inputs, and outputs.
- **Chief Secretary AI**: The orchestration agent that routes and consolidates work.
- **RBAC**: Role-Based Access Control.
- **RAG**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation.
- **MCP**: Model Context Protocol.
- **Membership**: A user's relationship to an organization.
- **Policy candidate**: A proposed issue or idea under evaluation.
- **Evidence item**: A source-backed fact, document excerpt, dataset, or citation.
- **Review state**: Draft, under review, approved, rejected, or archived.
- **Audit event**: A traceable record of a security- or governance-relevant action.
